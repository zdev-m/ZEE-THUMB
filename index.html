Frequently asked

Is this tool completely free?

Yes. ZEE THUMB is 100% free with no sign-up, no limits, and no hidden fees.

What URL formats are supported?

All standard YouTube formats: youtube.com/watch?v=…, youtu.be/…, youtube.com/shorts/…, embed URLs, and playlist URLs.

Why might Max Resolution not be available?

Not all videos have a maxresdefault thumbnail. In that case we automatically fall back to the next best available quality and indicate which one is shown.

Can I use downloaded thumbnails commercially?

Thumbnails are subject to YouTube's terms and the original creator's copyright. Always seek permission before commercial use.
